Logo Usage Guidelines

Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "F" in FundOps.

Minimum Sizes

Logo Type Minimum Width
Full Wordmark 120px
Short Mark (FOHQ) 32px

Do

  • Use on solid backgrounds (navy, white, light gray)
  • Maintain aspect ratio when scaling
  • Use appropriate variant for background color
  • Ensure adequate contrast

Don't

  • Rotate or skew the logo
  • Change the logo colors
  • Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
